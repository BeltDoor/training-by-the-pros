Who We Are

Your Neighbors. Your First Responders. Your Instructors.

At Training By The Professionals, we believe the best way to learn life-saving skills is from those who use them every day.

Our instructors aren't just certified teachers. They're active firefighters, EMTs, paramedics, and first responders who put these skills to work saving lives in our community. When you train with us, you're learning from professionals who have real-world experience in emergency situations.

Based in Munroe Falls, Ohio, we serve our local community with Red Cross certified training that prepares you to act confidently in any emergency.
